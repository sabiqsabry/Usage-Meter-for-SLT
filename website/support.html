Frequently Asked Questions

My usage is not updating?

Please try pulling down on the home screen to refresh the data. If the problem persists, ensure your internet connection is active and that the MySLT portal is accessible.

Can I add multiple accounts?

Once you are logged in, you can view the usage of all the connections registered under your MySLT account.

Is this the official SLT app?

No, this is a third-party application developed to provide a better native experience for iOS and macOS users.
