# Usage Meter for SLT

an iOS and macOS widget to peep your SLT broadband usage directly from your home screen.

## About the App

I got tired of logging into the portal just to check my remaining data. We all know how we feel about official SLT apps, so I made this. It's just a simple native app and widget built with SwiftUI to peep your broadband usage. No ads, no bloat.

[Download on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/lk/app/usage-meter-for-slt/id6757126471)

## Features

* Monitor your usage at a glance with home screen widgets for iOS, iPadOS, and macOS
* View multiple connections registered under a single MySLT account
* Your MySLT credentials are exchanged for a secure token, which is safely stored on your device (via Keychain)
* Logging in on one device signs you in across all devices using your Apple ID
* Currently supports SLT Fibre connections only
